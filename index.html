Welcome to my page. I am currently a PhD candidate at King's College London under the supervision of Georgios Chortareas and George Kapetanios. My research interests are primarily in macroeconomics and my thesis concerns the natural rate of interest.

Prior to my PhD, I read Economics at the University of Cambridge and Philosophy, Politics and Economics at the University of London. I have also taken postgraduate courses in advanced macroeconomics at the University of Oxford.
